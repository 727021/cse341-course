Overview

For this week's team activity, you'll have two options:

  1. Meet together to discuss the new technology that you researched.

  2. Create a livechat room using socket.io and sessions. You will be provided a tempalte, and this is easier than it sounds.

The second option is more suitable for those that do not have significant work remaining on their personal project, and wish to practice their skills more.

Option 1: Research a new web technology

Overview

Each member should take turns presenting the technology they learned about, what it is designed for, how it works, and it's strengths and limitations, etc.

At the end of this activity, each team member should write a short summary of their technologies presented (including their own and those of the other team members), and submit it to I-Learn.

Core Requirements

  1. Present your new technology to the team.

  2. Actively participate in learning from at least one other team member.

  3. Learn from ALL team members on the team.

Instructor Solution

Due to the nature of this assignment, there is no instructor solution. If you have discussed these technologies with your team, please mark "True" to the question about looking over the instructor solution.
