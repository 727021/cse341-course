Project 2 High-level Tasks

Overview

Your personal assignments of the second half of the semester are very flexible in how you implement them. You will only be graded on your final project submission.

To avoid falling behind, and to ensure successful and timely delivery of your project, it is recommended that you consider how you will spread out the six week's worth of work.

Many developers prefer to begin building the frontend of an application, while many others prefer to start with the database, then web services. You have the flexibility to create your application in the way that you prefer. The following are tasks in any application that will need to take place. You may choose to implement one per week depending on your preference.

High-level tasks to build your project, start to finish

  • App planning and risk analysis (completed in week 7)

  • Database (or data api) set up

  • REST service calls to read, update, and delete data

  • Frontend programming for appearance and to allow site navigation

  • Frontend functionality to read, update or delete data on pages

  • Authentication setup, implement form validation in app

  • Overall app refinement and testing
