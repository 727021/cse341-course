Instructor Preparation

This week is all about asynchronous JavaScript. There will be countless opportunities and use cases for asynchronous requests and functionality in web applications. The goal this week is to have our students understand well how this process works. They have worked with promises, callbacks, and asynchronous functions in Node quite a bit this semester. This week will focus on how to bring that understanding and those concepts to the frontend.
