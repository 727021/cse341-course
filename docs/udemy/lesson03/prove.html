Your assignment for this week will be to perform the following:

  1. Reorganize your personal project in such a way that it follows the MVS pattern.

  2. Create JSON file that has the data you need for your app.

  3. Create a webpage to get information from the json.

  4. Create the functionality to post new data (optional)

  5. Make sure your app has dynamic routes and paths that make sense for your app. One example is adding the specific ID of your product to your url path.
